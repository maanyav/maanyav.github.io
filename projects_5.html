DESIGN CONTROL PROCESS DIAGRAM - BellaFertility Pregnancy Kit

In this project, I explored the design control process for medical devices, focusing on the BellaFertility Pregnancy Kit. To simplify the complex workflow, I utilized Mermaid Live Editor, a code-based diagramming tool that allowed me to create and modify flowcharts quickly by typing simple text. This approach not only streamlined the process but also helped me avoid errors and concentrate more on the conceptual aspects of the design control process rather than getting bogged down in manual diagram creation.
